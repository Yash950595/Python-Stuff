The Best Anime According To Me

My Top 3 Anime of all time

Death Note

A high school student who discovers a supernatural notebook allowing him to kill anyone whose name he writes in it, leading to a thrilling battle of wits against a genius detective as the student creates a godlike vision of justice.

Blue Lock

All about how ego can transform an ordinary football player into a world class elite player.Only pure "egoist" survive here.

Solo Levelling

The greed for being the strongest with magical powers and continuous upgradation in ranks and domainance is all about here
